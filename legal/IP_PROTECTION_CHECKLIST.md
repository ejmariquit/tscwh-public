# IP Protection Checklist — TSCWH (Public Version)

This checklist summarizes high-level protection steps taken by the TSCWH
project. Detailed internal logs and sensitive metrics are not included in
this public repository.

- Copyright notices appear on all source files in the private codebase.
- A proprietary LICENSE file declares ownership and usage restrictions.
- Trade secret categories are described in general terms (e.g. safety stack,
  multi-agent council).
- Legal templates for NDA and pilot license are provided.
- Public materials share WHAT and RESULTS only; HOW descriptions are omitted.

For full protection details, refer to the private repository and consult with
legal counsel.
